What we know

The space describing itself. Every claim below is seeded from verified ground truth only — a live reproduction, a pre-registered robustness badge, a machine-checked theorem, or an honestly recorded gap — and carries its provenance: the job, badge row, file or chronology entry it traces to. Contested and superseded are visible states — the history of revision is part of the knowledge. Honest holes (what we cannot verify yet) are claims too. Nothing here is invented; confidence values are measured badge pass-rates, never assigned by hand.
