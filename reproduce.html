The SRI achieves AUC = 0.915 (excellent crisis discrimination). It sits modestly below the VIX benchmark (AUC 0.947, gap −3.4%) but warns earlier — 1-day lead vs the VIX's 2 days, with peak detection on 10 Mar 2020, one day before the WHO pandemic declaration.

Channels: Trade · Financial · Geopolitical · Behavioural · Monetary. Trade is empirically prominent across all post-2007 episodes (9% Pre-Crisis → 28% GFC, primary spec); the behavioural channel is bounded ≤22% under the de-confounded composite. Financial frictions dominate the Pre-Crisis baseline and the European Sovereign Debt Crisis; the remaining six episodes are method-sensitive and reported with explicit identification-status disclosure.
