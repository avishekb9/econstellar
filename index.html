About the lab
Econstellar is the research platform of the financial-econometrics group at the School of Humanities, Social Sciences & Management (SHSSM), IIT Bhubaneswar — led by Dr. Avishek Bhandari with Ipsita Parida. We build research that is open, reproducible, and runnable: every claim traces to a live computation on real data, and every result carries a permalink and a citation.
