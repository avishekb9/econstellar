Mission Brief

Dr. Avishek Bhandari operates from the Economics Research Station at IIT Bhubaneswar, where he studies how financial galaxies interconnect and evolve. His journey began at the University of Hyderabad, earning a Ph.D. while exploring "Interdependence, contagion and long-memory in global equity markets" through wavelet analysis—basically mapping the Force that binds market networks together.

Before joining the IIT Bhubaneswar outpost in late 2022, he spent time at IMT Hyderabad's research facility and completed specialized training at Oxford's macroeconomic academy. Think of it as Jedi training, but for understanding why markets do what they do when nobody expects them to.

His research universe spans financial econometrics, time series analysis, and the mysterious long-memory patterns that financial markets exhibit across different temporal dimensions. Recent expeditions include cryptocurrency network dynamics and how information flows through economic systems—because even the Death Star needed good communication protocols.
